# EasyFolio - Creative Developer Portfolio

EasyFolio is a professional, modern, and highly-responsive portfolio template designed for creative developers, designers, and freelancers. It provides a comprehensive platform to showcase your skills, work experience, projects, and services in a visually stunning and organized manner.

## 🚀 Key Sections

- **🏠 Hero Section**: Impactful landing with quick stats and clear Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons.
- **🙋 About Me**: Professional summary, personal information, and a digital signature for a personal touch.
- **📊 Skills**: Animated progress bars to visualize your technical expertise (HTML, CSS, JS, etc.).
- **📄 Resume**: A clean, vertical timeline for both professional experience and educational background.
- **📁 Portfolio**: Filterable project gallery with image lightboxes and detailed project links.
- **🌟 Testimonials**: Featured client reviews in a sleek, interactive slider.
- **🛠️ Services**: Comprehensive breakdown of core service offerings with intuitive icons.
- **❓ FAQ**: Accordion-style section to address common client inquiries.
- **📧 Contact**: Functional-ready contact form and info boxes for seamless communication.

## 🛠️ Built With

- **Bootstrap v5.3.3**: For a robust and responsive grid system.
- **AOS (Animate On Scroll)**: For smooth and engaging transition effects.
- **Swiper JS**: To power the high-performance testimonial slider.
- **GLightbox**: For a premium image and video viewing experience.
- **Google Fonts**: Modern typography (Roboto, Noto Sans, Questrial).
- **Bootstrap Icons**: For a consistent and clean iconography.

## 📁 Project Structure

- `index.html`: The main multi-section landing page.
- `portfolio-details.html`: Detailed view for individual projects.
- `service-details.html`: Comprehensive breakdown for specific services.
- `assets/`: Contains all CSS, JS, Vendor libraries, and Image assets.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Branding**: Open `index.html` and replace "EasyFolio" and "Eliot Johnson" with your own branding.
2. **Personalize Content**: Update the Resume and About sections with your unique career journey.
3. **Showcase Work**: Replace images in `assets/img/portfolio/` with your own project screenshots.
4. **Contact Info**: Update the email and phone details in the footer and contact sections.
